About Amanda

Nails! Nails! Nails!

Ballerina Life!

Fun In The Sun

UW'17

Amanda was born in New Haven, CT. As a young child she always loved being creative and expressing herself. She always loved dressing up, painting, drawing, and dancing. At the age of 3 she enrolled in ballet lessons, which lead to 21 years of exploring a career in dance. In 2012, Amanda decided to explore a new path in life and attend college at the University of Washington.

She is currently pursuing a double degree in Computer Science and Electrical Engineering. Her goal is someday to build robots that can help people overcome injury, disabilities, and other hardships. During her undergraduate studies, she loves to do nail art for the University of Washington community around her to express her creativity and be innovative.
