Who I Am

I am a senior at the University of Washington double majoring in Computer Science and Electrical Engineering. Before college, I spent the past two years training to become a professional ballet dancer. However, I decided to change career paths to attend the University of Washington because of the interest I have in innovative technology. My goal is to study robotics because I hope that, one day, I can use robotics to help those that might face limitations due to injury, lost limbs, or other causes.
